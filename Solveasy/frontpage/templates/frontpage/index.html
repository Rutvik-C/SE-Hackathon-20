Getting the best of both Worlds

Solveasy is a not for profit organization that is providing an interafce to connect local civic authorities and students. The district authorities can post their problems on the portal and receive solutions from students. These students can use these problems to create a project which they feel passionate about. This helps students in their academics as well as the civic bodies to solve their problems, thereby killing two birds with one stone.
